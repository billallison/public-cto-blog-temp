Pearl Project - 2020 Berkeley Changemaker Technology Innovation Grant Awardee [ARCHIVE]

Dec 8, 2020 Bill Allison

2020 Awardee Profile - Pearl Project

Awardee: Amin Jazaeri, Department of Physics

The Physics Department has also been thinking about the challenges of how to do social distance learning and do physics experiments. Physics Experiments Accessed Remotely Laboratory (PEARL) Project1 allows students to have real and physical interaction with laboratory equipment in real time as if they were present in the lab. Students who finish the course will then participate in designing the labs for future students. The funds will be used to design four initial experiments.

Pearl Project at the Fall 2020 Innovation Forum

  1. Formerly known as the Remotely Accessed and Controlled Experimentation Labs or RACE Labs. ↩︎
