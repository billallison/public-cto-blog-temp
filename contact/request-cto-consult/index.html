Request CTO Consult

The Office of the CTO offers consulting to help campus stakeholders steer towards effective technology solutions. To initiate a consultation, please fill out our request form (Calnet login required), or send an email to cto@berkeley.edu.

CTO Consulting Volunteers

I want to thank the many volunteers – from OneIT and beyond – whose time and dedication to helping the UC Berkeley community makes these consultations possible. They bring perspective and deep experience so we have more than one person’s perspective to help.
